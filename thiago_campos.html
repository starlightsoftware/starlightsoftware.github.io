Thiago Campos // CMO

15 de abril de 2019

The Explorer

Thiago Henrique Campos comes from Brazil to head our marketing department. At 35 (his next birthday will be on Sat, Jul 7, 2232 — in Pluto), he is in Porto studying the renowned Multimedia Masters at the Portuguese city’s university.

Background

With a background coming from a public office, Thiago is used to pressure. After a desk job helping pensioners, he founded one of the biggest iGaming websites in Brazil.

Just like that website came from leisure (instead of working), Thiago tells us that he likes volunteer work: he helped to create an app for fundraising and a website for a skating project for the youth in need.

Aboard Starlight Software

Thiago began his journey aboard our crew as a multimedia advisor. Now he is our CMO — Chief Marketing Officer —, responsible for all of our company’s communication strategy.

To the future

If he could go back in time, Thiago would tell his 10 year old self that “you’re not forced into anything. No one should take responsibilities that do not bring self-fulfillment”, he tells us.

When he grows up, he wants to leave a mark: a positive impact in people’s lives. His dream job is about “telling stories through images and letters”. How can you achieve your dreams? Making.

“Be makers! Transform your passions into purposes, making your abilities tools to move other’s lives.”
