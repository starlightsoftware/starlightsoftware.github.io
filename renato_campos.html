Renato Campos // Team Ingenuity

20 de maio de 2019

The Explorer

Renato Campos was a volunteer firefighter for seven years. Now, the scorching blazes were replaced by the high temperatures of chips and motherboards. Renato is on his way to be an engineer.

Background

Renato has no ordinary experiences. He was part of a team working with Formula 1 in schools and has entered competitions to complete both software and hardware challenges.

Aboard Starlight Software

Renato is working with Delloite, where he is responsible for the architecture of the software we are developing for our partner. He is discussing the project with the client and writing code for every front of the run.

To the future

Even though his dream was to be an astronaut, keeping his feet on the ground, Renato wants to finish his degree and start working. He recommends his peers to always keep up with the latest developments in the field — and to always have fun.
