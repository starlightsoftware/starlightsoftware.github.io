Team Quality

16 de abril de 2019

Have you ever wondered how everyday devices and machines, from temperature sensors to airplanes, are programmed?

Do you want to get into the ever more relevant area of Internet of Things by developing directly inside the “things” themselves rather than limiting yourself just to the cloud components?

Then look into embedded systems programming! Nowadays you can use a plethora of different languages, from the traditional C to the hipster Python, and frameworks such as Amazon Greengrass and FreeRTOS to program directly on the hardware and build solutions way beyond what a regular computer of smartphone could. You can take advantage of this resurgent area of programming and lift off towards the stars!
