Technology Career Advices NO ONE tells you

You’re in Charge of Your Career!

Nobody will ever care as much about your career as you will. At the end of the day it is up to you to make sure that you’re doing work that is meaningful and challenging.

Are you not growing? You’re Obsolete!

In any fast-moving industry, if you aren’t constantly picking up new skills, you’ll quickly become obsolete. You’ll need to take it upon yourself to continue evolving your skills.

Set realistic Goals!

Make realistic goals and get involved with people you admire. Learn to work hard and embrace setback as part of the process.

Networking, networking and more networking

Networking is one of the most crucial parts of building a career or business. Networking does not only mean that you must try to connect with someone you admire but also getting close with the people in the trenches as you evolve in your career.
